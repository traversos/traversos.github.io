Party Packages

Standard Packages • Pizza Packages • Custom Packages

Our party packages are available for groups of twenty or larger. Advance notice is required. All of our packages are served traditional family style, and are for dine-in only. An 18% gratuity will be added for parties of eight or larger. To be eligible for children's pricing, children must be under ten yrs of age. Children three and under are free.
