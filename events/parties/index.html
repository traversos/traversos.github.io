Party Packages

Standard Packages • Pizza Packages • Add-ons • Custom Packages

Our party packages are available for groups of twenty or larger. Advance notice is required. All of our packages are served traditional family style, and are for dine-in only. An 18% gratuity will be added for parties of eight or larger. To be eligible for children's pricing, children must be under ten years of age. Children three and under are free. Get 5% back in gift certificates for any party or catering order over $200 that’s paid in cash. Contact us for details.
